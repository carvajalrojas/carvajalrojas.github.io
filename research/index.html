Research

I'm currently interested in positive characteristic algebraic geometry and commutative algebra, with a focus on Fano geometry and the geometry of Frobenius endomorphisms. That is, I'm interested in Frobenius actions on Fano-type varieties and their singularities as well as objects and concepts defined via Frobenius actions, e.g. F-singularity theory, ordinarity, etc. My research concentrates on the study of Fano varieties and KLT singularities via globally F-regular varieties, strongly F-regular singularities, test and multiplier ideals, stringy motives, Kollár components, and étale fundamental groups. My research preprints.
